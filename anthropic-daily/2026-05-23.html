Anthropic's Project Glasswing logged 10,000-plus confirmed critical vulnerabilities and expanded its partner list; Claude Code pushed a quiet infrastructure update.

Lead

Ten thousand bugs.

Anthropic's Project Glasswing posted a milestone Friday: Claude Mythos Preview has confirmed more than 10,000 high-or-critical-severity vulnerabilities across production operating systems, browsers, and infrastructure software. (Benzinga, Anthropic) The program is expanding to additional partners beyond its founding roster: AWS, Apple, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorganChase, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Palo Alto Networks. Some partners reported bug-discovery rates more than 10 times their pre-Mythos baseline.

The number is notable. So is the framing: Anthropic is positioning this as a defender-first initiative, getting Mythos Preview's findings to the people who can patch them. That framing holds only as long as the output discipline does. On that question, the update is quiet.
