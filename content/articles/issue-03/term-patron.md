# Term of the Issue: Patron

> 100–150 word definition block for FORMULA.md term-of-issue slot. Earned by the week (per WIP 2026-04-30 PM editorial decision).

## Definition draft (research notes, prose Eddie's to finalize)

**Patron, in the Issue 03 sense:** the structural shape where compute flows in (Google $40B, AWS $100B, five gigawatts of TPU) and primitives flow out (Anthropic joins the Blender Development Fund as Corporate Patron, underwriting the Python API the connectors depend on). Patronage is not philanthropy. It is the relationship a generation of AI labs is now entangled in, with hyperscalers on one side and open-source software primitives on the other. The week of 2026-04-24 to 04-30 was the week the shape became visible from both directions in the same news cycle.

- Patron covers the full week — the compute deal, the connectors, the territory spread, the Disclosure Tax (because patronage is what the disclosure-tax critics are taxing). Surface only covers the product-distribution beat.
