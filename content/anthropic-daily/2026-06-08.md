### NEWS

**2026-06-08 — Apple WWDC 2026: iOS 27 Extensions include Claude as selectable AI model**
`NEWS`

Apple opened WWDC 2026 at its Cupertino campus on Monday with iOS 27, which introduces an Extensions framework letting users choose between multiple AI providers for Siri, Writing Tools, and Image Playground. Claude is in the initial lineup alongside Google's Gemini and OpenAI's ChatGPT. Providers integrate via their existing iOS apps; users who have one installed can designate it as their preferred AI engine for system-level features. When an outside model handles a Siri query, it responds in a distinct voice so users can tell which AI is answering.

Gemini holds the native integration slot, backed by a reported $1 billion annual agreement with Apple. Claude and ChatGPT enter as user-installed additions layered on top. iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 ship fall 2026.
