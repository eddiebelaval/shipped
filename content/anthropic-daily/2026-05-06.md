## Lead

The "Code with Claude 2026" keynote opened in San Francisco at 09:00 PT with CPO Ami Vora saying the event "is about how we are making our products work better for you." No new model. What shipped instead: Code Review, a tool that dispatches a team of parallel agents against a pull request, each checking for different error categories, leaving comments with conclusions and fix suggestions.
